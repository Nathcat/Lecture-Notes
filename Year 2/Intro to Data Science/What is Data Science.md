## Data Wrangling
This is the process of going from _raw_ data to _useful_ data. As an example, this might include removing punctuation from natural language.

A lot of time has to be spent on this initial wrangling to get the data into the shape you want.

## Data Cleaning
This is the process of detecting and rectifying data that is incorrect, incomplete, improperly formatted, or duplicated. This kind of _Dirty Data_ is often discovered during _Exploratory Data Analysis_.

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
This is a method of systematically going through the data without preconception, computing _summary statistics_, _plotting distributions_, and looking at _pairwise relationships_ between variables.
